Welcome to our student marketplace - Bazaar

Our Vision

Our mission is simple: to develop one that will provide an ease of access to a students’ buying and selling point within the students’ community. This perspective was formed by the authors’ own experience of hearing stories and wanted to create a tool that would make the student’s experience better, as well as help them feel connected to others. This WWW site forms the basis of a middleman, which fosters efficiency, and allows students to save their money as well as avoid unnecessary purchases, and to meet people from other groups.
